<p>On 2 April 2026, the school celebrated its 43<sup>rd</sup> anniversary
at an event graced by Venerable Dr. Shi Chuan Sheng, Vice-Rector (Academic
Affairs &amp; Advisor) and Associate Professor at the Singapore Buddhist
College as our Guest-of-Honour.&nbsp; He also currently serves as chairman
of the education committee of the Singapore Buddhist Federation and a member
of our school management committee (SMC).&nbsp; Other esteemed members
of the SMC, student award recipients, parents and teachers were in attendance.</p>
<p>‘<em>Building a Vibrant Learning Community’</em> was the chosen theme for
this year’s anniversary.&nbsp;&nbsp; In his speech, Venerable Chuan Sheng
highlighted the importance of nurturing life-long learners that are both
open-minded yet discerning, adaptive yet anchored to strong values to thrive
in an uncertain world.&nbsp; He believed that a vibrant learning community
is one where learning goes beyond the classroom — where students are encouraged
to think deeply, act with purpose, and contribute meaningfully to the world
around them.</p>
<p>One such school graduate who strongly embodied these qualities is Tay
Qi Xuan. &nbsp;&nbsp;She was conferred the Outstanding Manjusrian Award
2025 for her academic achievements (8 ‘O’ level distinctions) and all-round
excellence in areas such as demonstrated leadership abilities as a Student
Council Executive Committee member as well as upper secondary section/group
leader of Chinese Orchestra. &nbsp;&nbsp;She is now continuing her education
at Nanyang Junior College.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The event saw a parade of our uniformed groups as well as the preview
of items from the school public concert Arts Fiesta III which was being
staged at the Singapore Conference Hall later in April.&nbsp; Selected
performances by the Chinese Dance, Choir and Concert Band were showcased.&nbsp;&nbsp;
Mee Toh Foundation Arts scholarships, Kwan Im Thong Hood Cho temple scholarships
(Sports &amp; Uniformed Groups) and Alumni CCA awards (for Clubs &amp;
Societies) were presented to Manjusrians to support them in their studies
and furtherance of their talent or leadership in co-curricular activities.</p>
<p>The school congratulates all award recipients and wishes our graduates
every fulfilment in their continuing educational pursuits and future endeavours.</p>
