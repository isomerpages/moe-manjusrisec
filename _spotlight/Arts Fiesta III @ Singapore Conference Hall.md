<p>The 3<sup>rd</sup> edition of Manjusri Secondary’s Arts Fiesta was staged
at the Singapore Conference Hall on 11 April 2026 as an extension of our
44<sup>th</sup> school anniversary celebrations. This biennial public concert
showcased the performing arts talents of Manjusrians and was graciously
sponsored by the School Management Committee and Mee Toh Foundation.&nbsp;
Members of the management committee, current and former staff, alumni,
family and friends were in the audience to enjoy the occasion and support
our performers.</p>
<p>Included in the concert programme were performances by our Chinese Dance,
Chinese Orchestra, Choir, Concert Band and Wushu artistes.&nbsp; Also featured
were solo recitals by Li Yixian (Pipa), Tan Shao Long Aiken (Saxophone)
and Xia Zihan (Piano) as well as an alumni pop band performance by former
students (Estella Lim, Rachel Lian, Fu Xue Yi and Gun Jia Jun). &nbsp;&nbsp;The
event ended with a rousing combined singing of the school song played by
the Chinese orchestra.</p>
