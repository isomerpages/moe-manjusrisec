<p>4NT/4EXP/5N Graduated students who have been informed to collect their
graduation certificates may do so at the school’s General Office.</p>
<p>Details are as follows:</p>
<p>Venue: MJR General Office</p>
<p>Time: Monday to Friday, 9.00 a.m. – 5.00 p.m. (excluding public holidays)</p>
<p>Please remember to bring along a valid identification document for verification
when collecting your certificate.</p>
<p>If you are unable to come personally, you may authorise someone to collect
it on your behalf. The representative will need to bring a copy of your
identification document and a signed authorisation letter.</p>
<p>If you have any questions, please contact the school’s General Office.</p>
<p>We look forward to seeing you back in school!</p>
