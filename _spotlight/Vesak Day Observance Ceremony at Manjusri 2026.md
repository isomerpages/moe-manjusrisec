<p>Manjusri Secondary held its annual Vesak observance ceremony at the school
hall on 22 May.&nbsp; The school hosted a group of twenty seniors from
4S Active Aging Centre (Eunos Crescent) on the occasion.&nbsp; The seniors
enjoyed an arts and craft workshop session with student volunteers before
joining the audience at the hall for the ceremony.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
