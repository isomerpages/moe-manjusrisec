Students may connect to the school's SWN Wi-Fi network to access internet for learning purpose.  
  
1\. Go to 'Wi-Fi' under 'Settings' on your mobile phone.

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn1.png" style="width:40%">

2\. Select SWN@SSOE from the available Wi-Fi network list.

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn2.png" style="width:40%">

3\. Once connected, a login page will pop up.  
Log-in using your IAMS account. (Remember to check on the box to accept the terms.)

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn3.png" style="width:40%">

4\. If you have the certificates installed, you may press the 'Done' button. Otherwise, please proceed to step 5.

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn4.png" style="width:40%">

5\. If you are connecting to SWN on the mobile phone for the first time, please download and install the MOE-SSL certificate by tapping on "**Click here**". (You only need to do so once.)

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn5.png" style="width:40%">

6\. Press the 'Install' button when you see the 'Install Profile' page.

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn6.png" style="width:40%">

7\. For Android OS Phone, if your phone is not protected with a passcode, you will be prompted to create one. For IOS Iphone users, you will not be prompted to do this step.


<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn7.png" style="width:40%">

8\. Press the 'Install' button after you have entered your passcode.

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn8.png" style="width:40%">

9\. Press 'Done' to complete.

<img src="/images/Students%20and%20Parents/Swn%20wifi/swn9.png" style="width:40%">

**Note**: You cannot log-in to more than one device using the same account at the same time. When you log-in to a second device, the first device will automatically be logout.

To reset their IAMS password, please approach your form teachers to raise a request to ICT Department. Your IAMS password will expire every 90 days.
