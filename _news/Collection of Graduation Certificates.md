<p>The Singapore Graduation Certificates (SGC) are now ready for collection.</p>
<p>All graduates from the <strong>Class of 2025</strong>, as well as other
graduated students who have been informed to collect their certificates,
may do so at the school's General Office.</p>
<p><strong><u>Collection Details</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Venue:</strong> MJR General Office</p>
<p><strong>Collection Hours:</strong> Monday to Friday, 9.00 a.m. – 5.00 p.m.
(excluding public holidays)</p>
<p>Please bring along a valid photo identification document for verification
when collecting your certificate.</p>
<p>If you are unable to collect your certificate in person, you may authorise
a representative to do so on your behalf. The representative must present:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>A copy of your identification document; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A signed authorisation letter stating that you have authorised the named
person to collect your certificate on your behalf.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>If you have any enquiries, please contact the school's General Office.
<br>We look forward to welcoming you back to school!</p>
